Tondiarpet railway station

Tondiarpet Railway Station (TNP) is a key part of the Chennai Suburban Railway Network (SR), serving the Tondiarpet area, about 5 km north of Chennai Central. It functions as a busy junction for suburban lines and has connections for city transport (buses/taxis).
