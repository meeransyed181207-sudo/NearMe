VOC Nagar railway station

V.O.C. Nagar Railway Station (VOC) is a suburban station in Chennai, part of the Chennai Central-Gummidipoondi line, known for serving the Tondiarpet area and a large railway yard, often used by railway employees and railfans, with limited basic amenities but good bus connectivity to the city.
