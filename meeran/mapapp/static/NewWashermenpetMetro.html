New Washermenpet Metro

New Washermenpet Metro is a station on the Blue Line of the Chennai Metro. It is an elevated station that opened on February 14, 2021, and connects the northern suburbs like New Washermenpet with the Blue Line's southern terminals, such as Chennai International Airport.
