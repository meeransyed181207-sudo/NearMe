Apollo Hospitals Tondiarpet

Apollo Hospitals Tondiarpet Apollo Hospitals in Tondiarpet, Chennai, is a multi-specialty hospital established in 2000 to serve the North Chennai area. It is a 60-bed facility offering a wide range of services, including 24/7 emergency care, an ICU, an operating theater, and a dialysis unit.
